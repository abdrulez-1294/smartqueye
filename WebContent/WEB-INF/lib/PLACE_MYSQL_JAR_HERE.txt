PLACE YOUR MYSQL CONNECTOR JAR IN THIS FOLDER
=============================================

Download: https://mvnrepository.com/artifact/com.mysql/mysql-connector-j
  OR:     https://dev.mysql.com/downloads/connector/j/

File to download: mysql-connector-j-8.x.x.jar
  (Choose "Platform Independent" ZIP, extract the .jar file)

After placing the JAR here:
  Eclipse will automatically detect it on the build path
  (via the org.eclipse.jst.j2ee.internal.web.container classpath entry)

DO NOT place any other JARs here unless needed.
DO NOT rename the JAR file.

The project will NOT compile without this JAR.
